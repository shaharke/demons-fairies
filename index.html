The best 100 obscure rock acts 1968 1976
If you’re a fan of 60's and 70's music, I've got some great news for you!
Sure, everybody loves The Beatles, Led Zeppelin and Pink Floyd, BUT the famous names of the era are just the tip of the musical iceberg. There were more, way more simply amazing bands which just didn’t make it big.
WANNA KNOW WHO WERE THEY ?
Meticulously researched for years by 60’s-70’s Rock connoisseur Ra’anan Chelled, the book “Demons, Fairies & Wailing Guitars” compiles the finest 100 obscure bands from all the corners of the world and from all shades of Rock music in the years 1968-1976: 500 pages worth of Progressive Rock (Prog), Psychedelic Rock (Psych), Hard Rock, Blues Rock, Folk Rock, Singer-Songwriter, Krautrock, Acid Folk and more. It covers from semi known yet underappreciated bands like Hawkwind, MC5 and Nektar, through esoteric cult icons such as Rodriguez, Flower Travellin’ Band and Linda Perhacs, and right on to downright obscure acts like Josefus, Cressida and The Plastic Cloud. It also offers over 30 exclusive interviews not to be found anywhere else and many photos which add a personal touch to these often untold stories.

your guide to the uncharted musical territories of the era
Just read this book, put on your earphones and let your mind be blown.
